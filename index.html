Work Experience

Bank of America

As a 2017 summer intern, I worked on the network and architecture team. The bank had recently aquired a software defined networking tool called Nuage, and paired it with vSphere to move towards virtualization of datacenters. I was tasked with writing a python script that pulled data from the Nuage/vSphere environments and check whether it was setup correctly. Apart from the python CLI script, I also wrote a Flask web application that formatted the data and comparison in a readable fashion. The automation tool, Ansible, was also used to develop some parts of the project.


Cincinnati Children's Hospital

In the Summer of 2016 I completed the CCHMC SURF program that paired students with research labs within the hospital. I was placed in the allergy department and tasked with updating the front end of a website they were actively rolling out using Angular2-Meteor. The website is used for the analysis of next-generation sequencing data and the storage of results for 200-300 monthly experiments.

Personal Projects

Bubbl.io

Bubbl is a discovery and outreach tool designed to make finding influencers/experts in a specific niche and connecting with them easier than ever. By automating the process of finding influencers, maintaining and building a relationship with them, and doing outreach, the user can focus primarily on their marketing efforts.

Partify

Partify is a crowd sourced music playlist app that creates a democratic approach to choosing the songs played at a social event. Ususally, the person controlling the music gets to decide what mood the party leans towards. Partify aims to create a voting system on the next song that should play, making everyone have a say in what comes next.

VR Survival

VR Survival is a game that I created in my programming class druing my senior year in highschool. It was an open world survival genre, inspired by other games such as Minecraft and The Forest. I made the game compatible with the Oculus Rift, and presented it at the Association of Information Technology Professionals conference held at Ohio University, Athens, OH.
